Logistic Regression

Vector Notation one observation Matrix Notation all observations at once
Features
\(\vec{x}^{(i)} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1^{(i)} & x_2^{(i)} & \cdots & x_n^{(i)} \end{bmatrix}_{1 \times n}\)
\(X_{n \times m} = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} | & | & & | \\ \vec{x}^{(1)} & \vec{x}^{(2)} & \cdots & \vec{x}^{(m)} \\ | & | & & | \end{array}\right]\)
Weights
\(\vec{w} = \begin{bmatrix} w_1 & w_2 & \cdots & w_n \end{bmatrix}_{1 \times n}\)
\(W_{n \times 1} = \begin{bmatrix} w_1 \\ w_2 \\ \vdots \\ w_n \end{bmatrix}\)
Bias
\(b\)
\(B_{1 \times m} = \bigl[\,b\;\; b\;\; \cdots\;\; b\,\bigr]\)
Output Labels
\(y^{(i)} \in \{0, 1\}\)
\(Y_{1 \times m} = \bigl[\,y^{(1)}\;\; y^{(2)}\;\; \cdots\;\; y^{(m)}\,\bigr]\)
Logits
\(z^{(i)} = \vec{w}\cdot\vec{x}^{(i)} + b\)
\(Z_{1 \times m} = W^{\top}X + B\)
Model /
Prediction
\(\hat{y}^{(i)} = \sigma(z^{(i)})\)
\(= \dfrac{1}{1 + e^{-z^{(i)}}}\)
\(\hat{Y}_{1 \times m} = \sigma(Z)\)
(sigmoid is applied element-wise)
Loss
\(L\!\left(\hat{y}^{(i)}, y^{(i)}\right) = -\!\left[y^{(i)}\log\hat{y}^{(i)} + \left(1\!-\!y^{(i)}\right)\log\!\left(1\!-\!\hat{y}^{(i)}\right)\right]\)
\(= \begin{cases} -\log \hat{y}^{(i)} & \text{if } y^{(i)} = 1 \\[1ex] -\log\!\left(1 - \hat{y}^{(i)}\right) & \text{if } y^{(i)} = 0 \end{cases}\)
BCE (Binary Cross-Entropy) / Log Loss
\(L\!\left(\hat{Y}, Y\right) = -\!\left[Y \odot \log\hat{Y} + (\mathbf{1}_{1 \times m}\!-\!Y) \odot \log(\mathbf{1}_{1 \times m}\!-\!\hat{Y})\right]\)
BCE (Binary Cross-Entropy) / Log Loss
(log is applied element-wise)
Cost / Error
\(J(\vec{w}, b) = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m} L\!\left(\hat{y}^{(i)}, y^{(i)}\right)\)
\(J(W, B) = \dfrac{1}{m}\cdot\mathbf{1}_{1 \times m}\cdot L(\hat{Y}, Y)^{\top}\)
Cost Gradient
(weights)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial w_j} = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m}\!\left(\hat{y}^{(i)}-y^{(i)}\right) x_j^{(i)}\)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial W} = \dfrac{1}{m}\,X\!\left(\hat{Y} - Y\right)^{\!\top}\)
Gradient Descent
Update (weights)
\(w_j := w_j - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial w_j}\)
\(W := W - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial W}\)
Cost Gradient
(bias)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial b} = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m}\!\left(\hat{y}^{(i)}-y^{(i)}\right)\)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial B} = \dfrac{1}{m}\cdot\mathbf{1}_{1 \times m}\cdot\left(\hat{Y}-Y\right)^{\!\top}\)
Gradient Descent
Update (bias)
\(b := b - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial b}\)
\(B := B - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial B}\)
Regularisation Term
\(\dfrac{\lambda}{2m}\displaystyle\sum_{j=1}^{n} w_j^2\)
\(\dfrac{\lambda}{2m}\,W^{\top}W\)
Regularisation Gradient
\(\dfrac{\partial}{\partial w_j} \left( \dfrac{\lambda}{2m} \displaystyle\sum_{j=1}^{n} w_j^2 \right) = \dfrac{\lambda}{m} w_j\)
\(\dfrac{\partial}{\partial W} \left( \dfrac{\lambda}{2m} W^{\top}W \right) = \dfrac{\lambda}{m} W\)

Softmax Regression

Vector Notation Matrix Notation
one observation one observation all observations at once
Features
\(\vec{x}^{(i)} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1^{(i)} & x_2^{(i)} & \cdots & x_n^{(i)} \end{bmatrix}_{1 \times n}\)
\(X^{(i)}_{n \times 1} = \begin{bmatrix} x_1^{(i)} \\ x_2^{(i)} \\ \vdots \\ x_n^{(i)} \end{bmatrix}\)
\(X_{n \times m} = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} | & | & & | \\ \vec{x}^{(1)} & \vec{x}^{(2)} & \cdots & \vec{x}^{(m)} \\ | & | & & | \end{array}\right]\)
Weights
\(\vec{w}_c = \begin{bmatrix} w_{c,1} & w_{c,2} & \cdots & w_{c,n} \end{bmatrix}_{1 \times n}\)
\(W_{n \times K} = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} | & | & & | \\ \vec{w}_1 & \vec{w}_2 & \cdots & \vec{w}_K \\ | & | & & | \end{array}\right]\)
Bias
\(b_c\)
\(B_{K \times 1} = \begin{bmatrix} b_1 \\ b_2 \\ \vdots \\ b_K \end{bmatrix}\)
\(B_{K \times m} = \begin{bmatrix} b_1 & b_1 & \cdots & b_1 \\ b_2 & b_2 & \cdots & b_2 \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ b_K & b_K & \cdots & b_K \end{bmatrix}\)
Output Label
\(\vec{y}^{(i)} \in \{0,1\}^{K}\)
one-hot vector
\(Y^{(i)}_{K \times 1} = \begin{bmatrix} y_1^{(i)} \\ y_2^{(i)} \\ \vdots \\ y_K^{(i)} \end{bmatrix}\)
\(Y_{K \times m} = \left[\begin{array}{cccc} | & | & & | \\ \vec{y}^{(1)} & \vec{y}^{(2)} & \cdots & \vec{y}^{(m)} \\ | & | & & | \end{array}\right]\)
Logits
\(z_c^{(i)} = \vec{w}_c\cdot\vec{x}^{(i)} + b_c\)
\(Z^{(i)}_{K \times 1} = W^{\top}X^{(i)} + B\)
\(= \begin{bmatrix} z_1^{(i)} \\ z_2^{(i)} \\ \vdots \\ z_K^{(i)} \end{bmatrix}\)
\(Z_{K \times m} = W^{\top}X + B\)
Model /
Prediction
\(a^{(i)}_c = \operatorname{softmax}(\vec{z}^{(i)})_c = \dfrac{e^{z_c^{(i)}}}{\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{K} e^{z_k^{(i)}}}\)
\(\vec{a}^{(i)} = \begin{bmatrix}a_1^{(i)} & \cdots & a_K^{(i)}\end{bmatrix}\)
\(\displaystyle\sum_{c=1}^{K} a_c^{(i)} = 1\)
\(A^{(i)} = \operatorname{softmax}\!\left(Z^{(i)}\right)\)
\(A_{K \times m} = \operatorname{softmax}(Z)\)
softmax applied column-wise
Loss
\(L\!\left(\vec{a}^{(i)}, \vec{y}^{(i)}\right)\)
\(L\!\left(A^{(i)}, Y^{(i)}\right)\)
\(L\!\left(A, Y\right)\)
\(-\displaystyle\sum_{k=1}^{K} y_k^{(i)}\,\log a_k^{(i)}\)
\(= -\log a_c^{(i)} \quad \text{if } y_c^{(i)} = 1\)
Categorical Cross-Entropy
\(-\,{Y^{(i)}}^{\!\top} \log A^{(i)}\)
Categorical Cross-Entropy
\(-\mathbf{1}_{K \times 1}^{\top}(Y \odot \log A)\)
Categorical Cross-Entropy
Cost / Error
\(J\!\left(W, \vec{b}\right) = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m} L\!\left(\vec{a}^{(i)}, \vec{y}^{(i)}\right)\)
\(J\!\left(W, B\right) = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m} L\!\left(A^{(i)}, Y^{(i)}\right)\)
\(J\!\left(W, B\right) = -\dfrac{1}{m}\,\mathbf{1}_{K \times 1}^{\top}(Y \odot \log A)\,\mathbf{1}_{m \times 1}\)
Cost Gradient
(weights)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial w_{c,j}} = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m}\!\left(a_c^{(i)}-y_c^{(i)}\right) x_j^{(i)}\)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial W} = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m} X^{(i)}\!\left(A^{(i)}-Y^{(i)}\right)^{\!\top}\)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial W} = \dfrac{1}{m}\,X\!\left(A - Y\right)^{\!\top}\)
Gradient Descent
Update (weights)
\(\vec{w}_c := \vec{w}_c - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial \vec{w}_c}\)
\(W := W - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial W}\)
Cost Gradient
(bias)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial b_c} = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m}\!\left(a_c^{(i)}-y_c^{(i)}\right)\)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial B} = \dfrac{1}{m}\displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^{m}\!\left(A^{(i)}-Y^{(i)}\right)\)
\(\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial B} = \dfrac{1}{m}\,(A - Y)\,\mathbf{1}\)
Gradient Descent
Update (bias)
\(b_c := b_c - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial b_c}\)
\(B := B - \alpha\,\dfrac{\partial J}{\partial B}\)
